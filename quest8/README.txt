PROJECT TITLE: Piano Visualizer
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Show shapes while playing the piano.
VERSION or DATE: 10/24/2018
HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: Press run after compiling,
AUTHORS: Wise Guys
USER INSTRUCTIONS: Use the keyboard to play the piano.

Member Classes and visualization:
-Cole Tomaro: Triangle.java--triangles float upwards, change color, and spin when a key is pressed.
-Ben Smith: Hex.java--a cyan hexagon spawns and fades out when a key is pressed.
-Nick Anderson: Rect.java--a green rectangle spawns and fades out when a key is pressed.
-William Craig: Penta.java--a blue pentagon falls into the key pressed.
-Nathaniel Larsen: Oscillioscope.java--a big soundwave appears above the entire piano and moves upwards.

Member classes with arrays and the data contained:
-Cole Tomaro: arrays in Triangle.java for colors and X & Y coordinates for triangle corners
-Ben Smith: arrays in Hex.java for X & Y coordinates for the hexagon corners
-Nick Anderson: arrays in Rect.java colors
-William Craig: arrays in Penta.java for X & Y coordinates for pentagon corners
-Nathaniel Larsen: Oscillioscope.java--no arrays

Instructions to produce visualizations:
-Cole Tomaro: keyboard keys A, W, S & E
-Ben Smith: keyboard keys D, F, T & G
-Nick Anderson: keyboard keys Y, H, U & J
-William Craig: keyboard keys K, O, L & P
-Nathaniel Larsen: keyboard keys ; ' ] \
